Pancakes



Description

Pancakes are a delicious breakfast treat that can make any morning special. No need to worry about most of the ingredients, because most can already be found in your own kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt, or more to taste
  • 1 ¼ cups milk
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Sift the dry ingredients together.
  2. Make a well, then add the wet ingredients. Stir to combine.
  3. Scoop the batter onto a hot griddle or pan, suing approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.
  4. Cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry for about two to three minutes
  5. Flip and cook until browned on the other side.
